Mentioned as the adventure capital of south India,Dandeli is synonymous to peace and adventure.Dandeli is a splendid city of Karnataka.This place is situated at an altitude of 1551 ft above sea-level.An integral part of the western ghats,dandeli is home to colossal mountains,nature reserves,and fascinating wildlife. Dandeli is a complete package of fun,peace,and adventures.Dandeli's climate is subtropical, with a major influence of the western ghats on it.Temperature can stay between 20 and 42 degree celsius.The closeness to the Arabian sea affects the weather of this place.The post-monsoon season,october to january is the best time to travel to Dandeli.Temperature from september to november is also pleasant as it starts to cool down.
